A QUICK LOOK-SEE

Hello! My name is Tani, a graphic designer and web developer based in San Francisco, CA. I graduated from University of California Davis with Bachelor of Arts degrees in Design and Communication. After working as a graphic designer for a few years, I decided to dive deeper in tech and completed a 12 week immersive course on web development at General Assembly.

I currently work at an edtech startup in the Bay Area, doing both design and development. Being a graphic designer with a set of web development tools under my belt, I get excited for opportunities that allow me to interconnect my design and programming skills.

When not designing or strengthening my coding chops, you can find me outdoors, soaking up some rays with friends. From playing tackle football on the beach to night bike rides on the Embarcadero; having time away from the screen is always refreshing.

Sweatherman

Sweatherman allows users to retrieve weather temperature and conditions based on searched location. Users can also favorite specific cities and have them readily available in their profile. I enjoy the simplicity of the layout and the element of surprise when the weather data is displayed.

Project Image

Travelogue

Travelogue allows users to socialize about their travel adventures. Users can search by destination and blog about their experience and/or read what others have posted. This web application functions as a blog for travelers who want to share about their vacation experiences or get insight and opinions from others.
