A QUICK LOOK-SEE

Hey, Tani here. I'm a web developer x graphic designer based in San Francisco, CA. I graduated from University of California Davis with Bachelor of Arts degrees in Design and Communication. After working as a graphic designer for a few years, I decided to dive deeper in tech and completed a 12 week immersive course on web development at General Assembly.

I currently work at an edtech startup in the Bay Area, doing both development and design. Being a graphic designer with a set of web development tools under my belt, I get excited for opportunities that allow me to interconnect my design and programming skills.

When not designing or strengthening my coding chops, you can find me outdoors, soaking up some rays with friends. From playing tackle football on the beach to night bike rides on the Embarcadero; having time away from the screen is always refreshing.
